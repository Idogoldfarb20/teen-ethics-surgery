    <h2>About This Project</h2>

    <p>

      This platform helps teenagers understand the ethical reasoning behind cosmetic and reconstructive procedures. 

      It encourages self-reflection, responsible decision-making, and awareness of alternatives. Ido Goldfarb created this project to guide young people through safe and informed choices while understanding professional medical ethics.

    </p>

    <button class="collapsible">Read More About Ethics</button>

    <div class="content">

      <p>

        Ethical decision-making is when surgeons balance patient desires, physical safety, psychological impact, and professional responsibility.

        Sometimes the best choice is <span class="tooltip">refusal<span class="tooltiptext">Saying no to surgery can prevent harm or poor long-term outcomes.</span></span>.

      </p>

    </div>

  <section id="how-surgeons-think">

    <h2>How Surgeons Think</h2>

    <p>

      Surgeons consider many factors before operating: risk, long-term impact, patient expectations, and psychological motivation.

      Understanding this helps teens reflect on their own motivations and make safe, informed decisions.

    </p>

    <ul>

      <li>Patient expectations vs. realistic outcomes</li>

      <li>Long-term physical and psychological health</li>

      <li>Ethical and professional responsibility</li>

      <li>Alternative options or counseling</li>

    </ul>

  </section>
