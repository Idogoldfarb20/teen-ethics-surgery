About This Project

This platform helps teenagers understand the ethical reasoning behind cosmetic and reconstructive procedures. It encourages self-reflection, responsible decision-making, and awareness of alternatives. Ido Goldfarb created this project to guide young people through safe and informed choices while understanding professional medical ethics.

Ethical decision-making is when surgeons balance patient desires, physical safety, psychological impact, and professional responsibility. Sometimes the best choice is refusalSaying no to surgery can prevent harm or poor long-term outcomes..

How Surgeons Think

Surgeons consider many factors before operating: risk, long-term impact, patient expectations, and psychological motivation. Understanding this helps teens reflect on their own motivations and make safe, informed decisions.

  • Patient expectations vs. realistic outcomes
  • Long-term physical and psychological health
  • Ethical and professional responsibility
  • Alternative options or counseling

Safe Alternatives

  • Professional counseling for body image
  • Non-invasive procedures or temporary solutions
  • Healthy lifestyle, skincare, fitness improvements
